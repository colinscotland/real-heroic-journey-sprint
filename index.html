Questions you might have

Can I do this if I'm already in therapy? +

Absolutely. This work complements therapy beautifully. Therapy often explores the past; this Sprint focuses on the present disconnect and the future you're building. Many participants are in therapy simultaneously and find the two deepen each other.

What if I miss a session? +

Every session is recorded and available within 24 hours. While live attendance is ideal — the group dynamic is powerful — I've designed the experience so you can still get the full benefit from a recording.

Is this like other group coaching programs? +

No. This isn't rah-rah motivation or vision boarding. It's honest, rigorous, and grounded in neuroscience and positive psychology. Expect to be gently challenged, deeply heard, and held to your own truth. This is transformational work, not a pep talk.

What if I want to go deeper after the Sprint? +

Many people do. The Sprint often cracks something open that wants more space. After the Sprint, you'll have the option to continue with the full 4-month REAL Heroic Journey program or private 1:1 coaching. But there's no pressure — the Sprint stands powerfully on its own.

I'm a private person. Will I have to share everything? +

You share only what you choose to. The group operates as a confidential brave space, not a pressure cooker. Some of the most powerful shifts happen through listening, not speaking. You'll find your own rhythm.
